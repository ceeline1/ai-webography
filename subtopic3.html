1. California Miramar University \'96 Future of Artificial Intelligence

\ \ \ \ \
URLhttps://www.calmu.edu/news/future-of-artificial-intelligence/
AuthorCalifornia Miramar University
AbstractThis article from California Miramar University explores the anticipated advancements and trends in the field of artificial intelligence. It discusses how AI is poised to reshape a wide array of industries such as transportation, healthcare, education, and finance. The article explains how AI is expected to improve personalization, automation, and decision-making through data analysis. It also emphasizes the role of AI in smart cities, robotics, and autonomous vehicles. However, it highlights that ethical considerations must remain a priority, especially as AI systems grow in complexity and autonomy. The university argues that future success with AI will depend on thoughtful regulation, human oversight, and collaborative efforts to ensure safety, inclusivity, and trust in technological progress.
